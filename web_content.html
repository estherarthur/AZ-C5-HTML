West Hills Mall was opened in October 2014. It is the biggest mall not only in Ghana, but the whole of West Africa. The mall provides access to electronic, beauty, lifestyle, financial, pharmaceutical and healthcare services to people in the Dunkonah, New Weija vicinity and beyond. It also serves recreational needs and provides restaurants and eateries.

  • Fashion & footwear
  • Home & decor
  • Resturant & food
  • Service & speciality
  • Health & beauty


West Hills Mall is a 3-storey facility comprised of a basement, ground floor and first floor. The basement is comprised mostly of parking spaces and offices i.e. management & security offices. The ground floor is where most of the shops within the mall are located. A food court and a large car parking space is also located on the ground floor.
